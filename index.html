Making Grilled Cheese

What I need is:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese
    • Cheddar
    • Mozzarella
    • Pepper jack
  • 1 tbsp of butter

The steps to making grilled cheese are as follows:

  1. Spread butter on bread and frying pan
  2. Place bread in frying pan and fry
  3. Add cheese on top of bread
  4. Cover with second slice of bread
  5. Turn over and fry for 2 minutes

Congratulations..you perfectly made your grilled cheese
